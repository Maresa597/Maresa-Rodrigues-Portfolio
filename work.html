Project 01

WAI System Prototype

The Water Adaptive Intelligence (WAI) system is an innovative smart water management solution developed in the first semester of my study at UNASAT. The prototype integrates rainwater collection, filtration, real-time monitoring and small-scale energy generation to improve water efficiency and resilience. Developed in collaboration with my team, the project resulted in a fully functional prototype and was rewarded 2nd place at the UNASAT Science Fair 2026.

Technologies used

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript dashboard concepts
  • ESP32-S3 microcontroller
  • Water level and current sensor concepts
  • Prototype building and team presentation
